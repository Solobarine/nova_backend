# ğŸ“– Nova <a name="about-project"></a>

> Describe your project in 1 or 2 sentences.

This is the backend API for user Authentication for Nova, a web-based platform that allows users to explore and watch their favorite TV series. It provides a seamless experience for users to browse through a vast collection of series, view a list of genres, and access series based on their preferred genre. Users can access a wide range of TV series available on the platform.
Users can access a curated list of genres available on the platform. This feature allows them to explore series based on their preferred genre, such as drama, comedy, action, sci-fi, thriller, and more. Selecting a genre displays a list of series belonging to that genre.
